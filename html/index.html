About Me


I'm Rizwan Ahmed, a passionate web developer and freelancer based in Quetta, Pakistan. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, content writing, and C++. Currently, I'm pursuing a 4-year degree in Information Technology from the University of Balochistan.


With 2 years of experience, I've worked as an IT expert, office expert, team leader, solo worker, and full-stack developer. My expertise lies in creating dynamic and responsive websites, and I thrive on solving complex problems and bringing creative ideas to life through code.


When I'm not coding, you can find me playing cricket or traveling to new places. I believe in continuous learning and am always looking for new challenges.
